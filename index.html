What is the sum of integers from 1 to 10?

  1. 110
  2. 55
  3. 45
  4. 65

Comparing pair-wise sums \(1+10, 2+9, 3+8\)

We have pairs \((1,10), (2,9), (3,8),\dots\) all summed to 11. There are 5 such pairs. Thus, the result is 55.

Who discovered Gravity?

  1. Galileo
  2. Ampere
  3. Edison
  4. Newton

He discovered this after seeing a falling apple!

After seeing a falling apple, Newton questioned "What makes it falling down, not going up?" and later discovered that the attracting force that the Earth applied on the apple made it fallling down. This attracting force is called Gravity now.
